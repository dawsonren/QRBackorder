Computing Optimal Order Size and Reorder Point for Continuous and Periodic Review Inventory Systems

Businesses need to decide how much inventory to hold to avoid excess inventory costs while providing the right level of service to customers. The total costs are made up of the inventory cost, the order setup cost, and the backorder/lost sales cost. The service level is measured either by alpha (probability of all orders in a demand period fulfilled without delay) or beta (probability any given order is fulfilled without delay). Assume that leadtime is normally distributed.
